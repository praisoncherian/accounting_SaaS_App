Dan Issac Pothiyl, UK

Chartered Psychologist (BPS) | Clinical Psychologist (RCI)

Dan is a Clinical Psychologist accredited by the Rehabilitation Council of India, with over twelve years of post-qualification professional experience. He is also a Chartered Psychologist with the British Psychological Society (BPS).

He specializes in cognitive behavioural therapy (CBT) using a consistent motivational interviewing (MI) style, emphasizing schema change processes. He excels in clinical training and has extensive experience supervising clinical psychology trainees at the Rehabilitation Council of India recognized M.Phil. program.

Currently associated with CONNECT & RESTORE, he provides remote clinical supervision, training, and psychotherapy services. His academic experience includes serving as a Lecturer in Psychology at the University Centre at Blackburn College, UK, and presently as a Lecturer in Educational Psychology at Manchester Metropolitan University.
